PLTD optimisation opportunity

Many remote grids rely on diesel (PLTD). EnergyPOD can be deployed as a standardized hybrid add‑on to reduce diesel runtime and cost, while improving reliability and power quality.

Drop‑in hybridization

Add PV + BESS + PCS to existing diesel systems, shift energy to low‑cost solar, and use genset only when needed.

Standard SKU

Repeat procurement across provinces with consistent specs, testing, and O&M structure.

Data for budgeting

Monitoring & reporting helps PLN/ESDM track diesel savings, uptime, and performance — enabling better OPEX planning.
